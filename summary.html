Dyslexia

To give you some background dyslexia is a neurological conditions that primarily affects the skills involved in accurate and fluent word reading and spelling. Dyslexia is best thought of as a continuum, not a distinct category, and there are no clear cut-off points. Those with dyslexia will be affected differently but some of the common areas are as follows:

  • Reading
  • Writing
  • Spelling
  • Squencing

It is important to understand that not every aspect of dyslexia is bad, and sufferers often excel in other areas. Common strengths that those with dyslexia display are as follows:

  • Innovative thinkers
  • Excellent trouble shooters
  • Intuitive problem solving
  • Creative in may ways
  • Lateral thinkers
